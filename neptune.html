Solar System Explorer
NEPTUNE

Neptune is the farthest planet from the Sun, making it extremely cold and dark. It has a deep blue color due to methane in its atmosphere. Neptune has the strongest winds in the Solar System, reaching 2,100 km/h (1,300 mph).
It experiences huge storms, including the Great Dark Spot, similar to Jupiter’s Red Spot. Neptune’s moon Triton orbits backward, suggesting it was captured from the Kuiper Belt. Scientists believe Triton has an underground ocean beneath its icy surface

  • Neptune has 14 known moons, with Triton being the largest.
  • A year on Neptune lasts 165 Earth years.
  • It has faint ring systems made of dust and ice.
