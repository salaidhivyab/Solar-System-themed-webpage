EARTH

Earth is the only planet known to support life, thanks to its breathable atmosphere and abundant water. About 71% of its surface is covered in oceans, which help regulate climate and sustain life.
The planet experiences tectonic activity, shaping mountains, valleys, and ocean basins. Earth’s magnetic field protects it from harmful solar radiation. The ozone layer absorbs most of the Sun’s UV rays.

  • Earth rotates once every 24 hours, creating day and night.
  • It has one moon, which affects ocean tides.
  • Its diverse ecosystems range from polar ice caps to tropical rainforests.
